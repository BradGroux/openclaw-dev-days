# Market Researcher Prompts

## Starter Prompts
- Research this app idea and identify 3 direct competitors, 3 adjacent products, and the strongest UX patterns we should study.
- Find examples of products with strong progress, rewards, or checklist loops that could inspire this app.
- Compare how similar apps position their value. What language keeps repeating, and where can we sound clearer?
- For a workshop MVP, tell me what feels table stakes in this category versus what we should ignore.
- Look at these 2 reference apps and tell me which patterns are worth borrowing for our product.
