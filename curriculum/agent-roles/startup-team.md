# Startup Team Agent Roles

## Core Team

- **Market Researcher** — finds adjacent products, patterns, competitors, and market framing
- **Idea Validator** — pressure-tests value, scope, assumptions, and MVP logic
- **Coder** — scaffolds, builds, fixes, and iterates on the app
- **Project Manager** — breaks work into milestones, tracks scope, and sequences execution
- **Outreach Assistant** — writes positioning, social copy, launch language, and follow-up messaging

## The Orchestrator

The orchestrator is the human-guided lead who decides what to delegate, evaluates outputs, and keeps the team aligned.
